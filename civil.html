Civil Engineering Department

The Civil Department trains future engineers in structural, geotechnical, transportation, and water resources engineering, preparing them for real-world infrastructure development.

We emphasize hands-on experience and sustainable engineering practices.

  • Year of establishment: 2014
  • Head: Md. Naimul Haque (Chairperson & Associate Professor)
  • Programs offered: Undergraduate B.Sc. (Hons.) in Civil Engineering; Graduate (M.Sc.) programs in Civil engineering
  • Accreditations/affiliations: Follows an Outcome-Based Education curriculum as recommended by BAETE and UGC. (BAETE-accredited, UGC-approved.)
