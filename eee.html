Electrical & Electronic Engineering (EEE) Department

The EEE Department offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Electrical & Electronic Engineering, covering power systems, electronics, and telecommunication.

Our curriculum prepares students for careers in diverse fields including power generation, automation, robotics, and communication technologies.

  • Year of establishment: January 2005
  • Head: Khairul Alam, PhD (Chairperson & Professor)
  • Programs offered: Undergraduate B.Sc. (Hons.) in EEE; Graduate M.Sc. in EEE
  • Accreditations/affiliations: The B.Sc. in EEE program is BAETE-accredited (under IEB), ensuring it meets national engineering education standards. (UGC-approved.)
