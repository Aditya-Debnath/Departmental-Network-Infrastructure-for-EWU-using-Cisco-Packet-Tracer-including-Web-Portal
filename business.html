School of Business Administration

Our Business School offers BBA, MBA, and Executive MBA programs focusing on leadership, marketing, finance, HR, and management practices in a global environment.

We aim to develop ethical and effective business leaders equipped to tackle modern challenges.

  • Year of establishment: 1996
  • Head: Dr. Farhana Ferdousi (Chairperson & Professor)
  • Programs offered: Undergraduate B.B.A. (Honors); Graduate M.B.A. and E.M.B.A. (Evening MBA). Emphasis on strategic, managerial and leadership skills
  • Accreditations/affiliations: EWU’s Business programs have earned ACBSP (USA) accreditation for its Faculty of Business & Economics, assuring international quality standards. (UGC-approved.)
