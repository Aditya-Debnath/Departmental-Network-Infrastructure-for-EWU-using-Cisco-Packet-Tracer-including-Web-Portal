Department of Pharmacy

EWU’s Pharmacy Department delivers B.Pharm and M.Pharm degrees, emphasizing clinical pharmacy, pharmaceutical technology, and pharmacology.

Our programs prepare graduates for careers in community pharmacy, hospital pharmacy, the pharmaceutical industry, and research.

  • Year of establishment: 2003 (The Pharmacy Council of Bangladesh accredited the Department of Pharmacy, East West University on August 13, 2007.)
  • Head: Dr. Md. Amirul Islam (Chairperson & Professor)
  • Programs offered: Undergraduate B.Pharm (Honors) (4-year program); Graduate M.Pharm, including specializations in Clinical Pharmacy and Molecular Pharmacology
  • Accreditations/affiliations: pproved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by the Pharmacy Council of Bangladesh, ensuring compliance with national pharmaceutical education standards.
