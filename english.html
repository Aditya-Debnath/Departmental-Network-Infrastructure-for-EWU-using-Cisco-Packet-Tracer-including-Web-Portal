Department of English

The English Department offers undergraduate and graduate programs in English Language, Literature, and Applied Linguistics to develop academic and professional communication skills.

Students engage critically with diverse texts and theories, enhancing their analytical and expressive abilities.

  • Year of establishment: August 1996 (This was the third founding department.)
  • Head: Dr. Akhter Jahan (Chairperson & Associate Professor)
  • Programs offered: Undergraduate B.A. (Honors) in English; Graduate M.A. in English, M.A. in TESOL. These programs cover English literature and applied linguistics.
  • Accreditations/affiliations: UGC-approved arts programs.
