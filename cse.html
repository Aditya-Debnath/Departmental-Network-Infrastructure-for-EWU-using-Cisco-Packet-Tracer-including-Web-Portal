Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) Department

Welcome to the CSE Department at East West University. Our programs include B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering and postgraduate studies in computing and IT infrastructure.

We focus on cutting-edge research areas like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Software Engineering.

  • Year of establishment: 1996
  • Head: Dr. Maheen Islam (Chairperson & Associate Professor)
  • Programs offered: Undergraduate B.Sc. (Hons.) in CSE; Graduate M.Sc. in CSE
  • Accreditations/affiliations: The CSE program is BAETE-accredited (Institute of Engineers Bangladesh) for 4 years from July 2022, reflecting compliance with Bangladesh engineering education standards. (All programs are UGC-approved.)
