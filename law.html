Department of Law

The Law Department offers LLB and LLM programs, empowering students with legal expertise, critical analysis, and ethical practice for the judicial sector and corporate world.

Our faculty includes experienced legal scholars and practitioners dedicated to fostering a strong understanding of justice and legal systems.

  • Year of establishment: September 2014
  • Head: Dr. Nabaat Tasnima Mahbub (Chairperson & Associate Professor)
  • Programs offered: Undergraduate LL.B. (Hons) - Bachelor of Laws (4-year program); Graduate LL.M (Master of Laws). Both programs meet professional legal education requirements.
  • Accreditations/affiliations: UGC-approved law programs. Often curricula are overseen by Bangladesh Bar Council standards.
